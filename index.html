The Problem We're Solving

Immigrant students face unique barriers that prevent them from pursuing entrepreneurship and realizing their full potential.

73% of immigrant students lack access to entrepreneurial mentorship and networks
85% view entrepreneurship as too risky without family financial backing
92% of schools lack hands-on entrepreneurship programs for students
