Phase 1

The Bootcamp

4 weeks of intensive entrepreneurship training

Week 1

Ideation, Solutions, and Business Models

Guest Mentor: Prithiviraj Logan

We led students through ideation, solution framing, and the fundamentals of business models. Our guest mentor Prithiviraj Logan supported with insights on starting with a niche marketâ€”identifying a specific group of people with a specific problem before scaling.

Week 2

Finance & Marketing

Guest Mentor: Ross Gillispie, Banker

We taught students financial planning and marketing strategy, while guest mentor Ross Gillispie shared real-world insights from banking. Students learned how to price, project revenue, and position their ideas to reach the right audience.

  • Financial planning & budgeting
  • Pricing strategies
  • Revenue projections
  • Marketing fundamentals
Week 3

Pitch Deck Development

Guest Mentor: Srinivas Maram

We led students through building their pitch decksâ€”crafting a strong narrative, clarifying the problem and solution, and presenting their market and traction. Guest mentor Srinivas Maram provided feedback on structure, clarity, and how to tell a compelling story.

  • Pitch deck structure & flow
  • Problem-solution storytelling
  • Market & traction framing
  • Clear ask & next steps
Phase 2

The Pitch Competition

Top 10 students selected by us compete for prizes and recognition

In Week 4, we selected the top 10 students based on their dedication, progress, and business potential. These finalists presented their fully-developed business ideas to a panel of judges and mentors, showcasing everything they learnedâ€”from problem identification to financial planning.

The competition was fierce, with each team delivering compelling pitches. The top 3 winners walked away with cash prizes and validation that their ideas had real potential in the market.

  • Top 10 finalists selected by Project Origin
  • Live judging and Q&A
  • Cash prizes and recognition

Phase 3

Exclusive Webinar with Shashank Saxena

$510M exit founder provides personalized feedback

The top 3 students plus 7 of the best immigrant student founders across America that we scouted earned an exclusive webinar with Shashank Saxena, a successful VC who sold his company VNDLY to Workday for $510 million.

During this private session, Shashank provided personalized advice and feedback on each team's business, sharing insights from his journey building and scaling a successful startup to a massive exit. This was a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for our students to learn from someone who has achieved what they aspire to.
